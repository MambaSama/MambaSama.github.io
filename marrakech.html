About my CSS

The original CSS painting was by Frank Stella Frank Stella, Marrakech . As a Part of Stella's Morocco pieces, the Marrakech was meant to not only be a variation of an Arabic Tile, but to be an illusion. It creates a pattern of depth and movement. In my recreation of it, I think I was able to capture the same goals as Stella. However, I could have just been looking at it much too long now, but I think it does give an effect of movement. I hope that the viewers of my piece, as much as the viewers of Stella's piece, get taken into the movemnet of it and come to realise that this is also 117 different divs in this piece.
